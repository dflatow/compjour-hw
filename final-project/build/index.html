The Hillary Clinton Benghazi Emails

The purpose of this webpage is to create a more user-friendly way of exploring the corpus of documents released by Hilary Clinton to the State Department. Currently, the easiest way to explore these emails is via navigating corpus of pdfs with limited search, sort, or visualization functionality. We not only improve the search and sort capabilities here, but also present some visualizations to give a sense of the dynamics of Hilary's emailing behavior over the period reported in the form of calendar and wordcloud plots. Further, there is a seperate page for each email (links in the table at the bottom)
